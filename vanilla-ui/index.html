    Quick Start

    1. Add folders: Click "+ Add Folder" in Library to add your music directories.
    2. Scan: Click "Scan Library" to index all tracks from your folders.
    3. Connect USB: Go to USB and select your External library USB drive.
    4. Create playlist: Click "+ New" in the sidebar to create a playlist, then add tracks from Library or USB.
    5. Export: Open your playlist and click "Export to USB" to write it to your drive.
